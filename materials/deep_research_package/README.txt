Deep research package for the presentation based on:
“Women's connectivity in extreme networks” (Manrique et al., 2016)

What is included
- report/01_deep_research_report_reconstructed.md
  Reconstructed export of the deep research report content, organised for easy reuse.
- report/02_slide_inventory.csv
  Compact slide list with timing and primary visual.
- figures/
  Extracted composite figures from the uploaded paper plus the full page images used for extraction.
- paper/
  The uploaded source PDF.

Important note
- Per your clarification, this package does NOT try to compile or validate a LaTeX project.
- The report text is a faithful reconstruction of the deep research report’s content and structure,
  but I did not attempt a byte-for-byte export of the widget output.
- The three main paper figures were extracted from the uploaded PDF as composite images.
